| Basal Ganglia (BG) | Variable | Baseline | Minimal | NS |

**Pattern:** Cingulate gyri and parietal regions show **earliest metabolic injury**

#### 2. HIV-Specific Neurotoxicity

**HIV-1+ vs HCV+ Comparison:**

| Metabolite | HIV-1+ | HCV+ | Interpretation |
|-----------|---------|------|----------------|
| NAA/Cr | **↓↓↓ (p<0.05)** | ↔ (NS) | HIV-specific neuronal injury |
| Cho/Cr | ↑ (trend) | ↔ (NS) | HIV inflammatory effects |

**Key Point:** Both HIV and HCV cause systemic inflammation, but **ONLY HIV shows CNS metabolic injury** → Direct neurotoxicity beyond inflammation
