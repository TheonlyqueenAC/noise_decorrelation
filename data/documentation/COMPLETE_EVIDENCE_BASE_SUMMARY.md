## THE PARADOX IN STARK CONTRAST

### ACUTE HIV: Protected Despite Crisis

**Valcour 2015 (n=44 individual patients):**
- Viral load: ~227,000 copies/mL (untreated)
- CD4: 446 cells/μL
- CNS invasion: 88% detectable CSF viral load
- **NAA: +9.0% ELEVATED vs controls (p=0.016)**
- **Cognition: 68.9% normal**

### CHRONIC HIV: Injured Despite Treatment

**Bladowska 2013 (n=32 neurologically asymptomatic):**
- Viral load: SUPPRESSED (on cART)
- CD4: Variable, on treatment
- Clinical status: Asymptomatic
- Structural MRI: COMPLETELY NORMAL
- **NAA: -10-20% REDUCED vs controls (p<0.05)**
- **Metabolic injury present despite treatment**

### The Core Insight

**Acute mechanisms (crisis response) > Chronic mechanisms (long-term compensation)**

Treatment doesn't restore acute-phase protection. Your framework explains why.
